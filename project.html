$ P \rightarrow Q, P \vdash Q $ Modus Pones
$ P \rightarrow Q, \neg Q \vdash \neg P $ Modus Tollens
$ P \rightarrow Q, Q \rightarrow R \vdash P \rightarrow R $ Hypothetical Syllogism
$ P \rightarrow Q \wedge R \rightarrow S, P \vee R \vdash Q \vee S $ Constructive Dillema
$ P \rightarrow Q \wedge R \rightarrow S, \neg Q \vee \neg S \vdash \neg P \vee \neg R $ Destructive Dillema
$ P, Q \vdash P \wedge Q $ Conjunction
$ P \vdash P \vee Q $ Addition
